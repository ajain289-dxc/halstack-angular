If the colors of a component's theme do not match your wishes or requirements, you can modify them in order to customize the component. The ThemeModule, ThemeService and the directive theme are all provided by the CDK and they need to be inserted in your app module and HTML component, as in the example below.

ThemeService is the service that will be registering your custom theme in your app and ThemeModule is the module that allows this service to work.

Please follow the next steps to add your own theme:

  1. Provide ThemeService and import ThemeModule in your app module.
  2. To create your theme object, you should declare the theme inputs for each component, which are documented in the themes guidelines. Values for the theme inputs must be in color HEX codes.
  3. Inject ThemeService and register your theme in app.component.ts. This will be done with the help of the exported register(theme: Theme) function.
  4. Include the directive theme in your HTML component.
